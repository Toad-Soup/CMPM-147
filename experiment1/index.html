Experiment 1 - Hello Glitch/ Generator

Translated from bad-name-generator- on glitch.com

Description

I made a Random Band Genrator using Glitch.com! It will generate the name of a band, the genre, and aesthetics of the band all for the user!

Technical

I followed Prof. Wes Modes example of creating a bunch of tokens and having a base template, where the tokens are randomly slected to fill in the template, Mad Libs style!

Reflection

This is my first time working with JS and HTML so i'm a little unsire of myself. There is more I wish I could have done, such as creating band member names based on the randomly selected aesthetic. But I'm not that advanced yet so maybe one day in the future!
